Membership Tiers

There are three membership tiers available to organisations: Premium Members get a guaranteed seat at the board, directly influencing the direction of the growth through funds allocation. General Members elect one representative at the board as a class. Affiliate Members can join for free but have no voting rights.

You can review the Membership Agreement beforehand (this is for review only, not for signing). You can also read the Articles of Association, the Rules of Operations, and the Good Standing Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at foundation@sel4.systems.
