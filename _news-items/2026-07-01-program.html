The program of the seL4 Summit 2026 is now available!

The 2026 edition of the seL4 summit features a full first day dedicated to high-level overviews and perspectives, followed by Days 2 and 3 focusing on more technical insights and updates. Have a look at the [abstracts] from our exciting keynotes and voices-from-nearby presenters, and the great line-up of interesting seL4-related research, development, field reports and technical discussions.

The event will offer an ideal opportunity for people new to seL4 and to formally verified systems to increase their understanding of the landscape and ecosystem, and meet with seL4 developers, adopters and enthusiasts. Don't forget to register and see you soon in Vancouver.
