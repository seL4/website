We are pleased to welcome a new member - Gapfruit - to the seL4 Foundation.

Gapfruit is a Swiss technology company building trustworthy foundations for the systems society depends on - from industrial controls to energy infrastructure. To achieve this, Gapfruit combines microkernel operating systems with capability-based security and hardware-rooted trust - an approach grounded in standards from the Trusted Computing Group. Together, these technologies enable resilient, verifiable device operations for the critical systems that underpin modern infrastructure. By joining the seL4 Foundation, we aim to advance high-assurance computing in critical infrastructure and collaborate with the global seL4 community to make trustworthy systems the standard - not the exception.
