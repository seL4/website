The seL4 Foundation is pleased to welcome MEP as a Member. MEP is a voice communications supplier for Air Traffic and Maritime critical communications worldwide. We are using seL4 in the heart of our Voice Communication System, the SureVoice Solid. MEP has chosen for process separation within the seL4 to achieve 24/7 availability.
